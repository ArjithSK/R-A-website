About Robotics and Automation Association

Our Vision

The Robotics and Automation Association is committed to advancing the field of robotics and automation technologies through collaboration, education, and innovation. We strive to create a platform where professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts can exchange ideas and drive technological progress.

Our vision is to shape the future of automation by fostering interdisciplinary connections and promoting responsible development of robotic systems that enhance human capabilities and improve quality of life.

Key Initiatives

Our association focuses on a range of activities to support the robotics and automation community:

  • Industry-academia collaborative projects
  • Professional development and certification programs
  • Annual conferences and technical symposiums
  • Publication of research journals and technical reports
  • Advocacy for responsible automation policies

We provide members with access to specialized resources, networking opportunities, and platforms to showcase innovative solutions in robotics and automation.

Our Legacy

Established in 2008 by leading experts from industry and academia, the Robotics and Automation Association has grown into a global community with over 5,000 members across 45 countries. Our collective expertise spans industrial automation, service robotics, AI, and human-robot interaction.

Throughout our history, we've helped establish industry standards, influenced automation policy, and fostered innovations that have transformed manufacturing, healthcare, agriculture, and numerous other sectors.
