About Us

A four-year B.E UG programme on Robotics and Automation is launched in the silver jubilee year of the institution with an intake capacity of 60 students. It's a collaborative programme with Ariel University, Israel and L&T Technologies Services, Chennai and Craftsman Automation, Coimbatore are Industrial Knowledge partners for the programme. Robotics and Automation programme focuses on the design, construction, operation and use of autonomous and robotic device.
